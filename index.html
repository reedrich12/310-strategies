Partnership Model

A Low-Risk Path to AI-Native Learning

We embed within your institution to build, launch, and scale accredited AI-enabled programs — without upfront costs. Your institution experiments with cutting-edge learning models and generates long-term revenue.

01
Establish a New Entity
We develop a new entity within your institution — such as a branch campus or program division — handling all accreditation matters.
02
Design AI-Native Systems
New systems for instructional delivery and assessment, aligned with your learning outcomes and accreditation standards.
03
Launch, Market & Recruit
We deliver the learning experience and handle all marketing and recruitment. No new teams or infrastructure needed.
04
Ensure Compliance
Ongoing compliance with accreditation standards, working alongside your institutional staff to maintain quality and integrity.
05
Transition to Independence
The branch campus becomes independent. Your institution shares revenue for the next 15 years — a sustainable income stream.
